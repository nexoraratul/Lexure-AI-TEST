Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this offline?

Yes! After the initial model download, Lexure AI works completely offline. Perfect for privacy and areas without internet.

Is my data really private?

100% private. Your device processes everything locally. No messages go to any server or cloud service.

How large is the model?

About 500MB for our current model. After download, it stays in your browser cache for instant offline use.

Can I export my conversations?

Yes! Export as TXT or JSON anytime. You own all your data and can delete it whenever you want.

What model does Lexure use?

Llama 3.2 1B Instruct model via WebLLM. Open source, efficient, and runs on modest hardware.

Will there be a paid version?

The local mode is and will always be free. Pro Cloud Mode with bigger models is coming soon as optional.
