Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Our Privacy Promise

Lexure AI is built on a simple principle: Your privacy is paramount. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or conversations to any external servers.

2. How Lexure AI Works

Lexure AI runs entirely in your browser using WebLLM technology. This means:

  • Your AI model (Llama 3.2) runs on your device, not on our servers
  • Your messages are processed locally in your browser
  • No data is sent to any cloud service or third-party API
  • Your conversations exist only in your device's local storage

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Your chat messages or conversation history
  • Personal information about you
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking information
  • Memory or facts you save about yourself

4. Local Storage Only

All your data stored by Lexure AI is kept exclusively in your browser's IndexedDB:

  • Chat history and conversations
  • Memory (facts about yourself)
  • Settings and preferences
  • Feedback and reactions

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

5. Offline Functionality

After the initial model download, Lexure AI works completely offline. You can use the service without an internet connection, further ensuring complete privacy and data protection.

6. Open Source Model

Lexure AI uses Meta's Llama 3.2 model, which is open source. This means:

  • The model code is publicly auditable
  • No hidden proprietary algorithms
  • Complete transparency in how AI works

7. Third-Party Services

Lexure AI does not integrate with or send data to:

  • OpenAI, Google, or other AI API providers
  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any tracking or monitoring services

8. Data Export & Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export conversations as TXT or JSON anytime
  • Delete individual conversations
  • Clear all data with one click
  • Delete memory entries selectively or entirely

9. Browser Storage Limitations

Note that browser storage has typical limitations:

  • Clearing browser data/cache may delete stored conversations
  • Storage limits vary by browser (typically 500MB-1GB)
  • Some browsers allow persistent storage with user permission
  • Export conversations regularly for backup

10. Future Pro Cloud Mode

In the future, we may offer optional "Pro Cloud Mode" for:

  • Access to larger, faster models
  • Cloud synchronization across devices
  • Advanced features

If implemented, Pro Cloud Mode will be entirely optional and will have a separate, clear privacy policy. The free local mode will remain always available with the same privacy guarantees.

11. Security

Your security is important to us:

  • No transmitted data = no network vulnerabilities
  • Browser's built-in security protects local storage
  • Disable JavaScript to ensure no data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates

12. Policy Changes

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will notify users prominently. Any changes will not retroactively affect previously stored data without explicit user consent.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our GitHub repository or visit our homepage.

Bottom Line: Lexure AI is designed for privacy. Your conversations, your data, your device. No exceptions.
